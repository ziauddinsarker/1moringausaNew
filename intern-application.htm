How This College Internship Works

This is an unpaid position.

We are looking for one intern or "Campus Ambassador" per school.

You set your own hours.

This position ends September 15th, 2013. At that time we will be hiring new interns for a period of one year.

If you can provide us with the information we request below and you are the first one from your school to apply, you will be hired. Once hired you will be given your own identifying "code" which students will use to get a $5 discount off their purchases.

We will track your sales in relation to other interns/Ambassadors at colleges all around the nation.

Periodically, every month or so, we will send out the results of intern generated sales via email. If you are in the top 10%, 20%, or 30% of all sales compared to the other interns around the country that is something to be very proud of. All interns will receive a short letter of recommendation. But those who land on top will get a letter of recommendation stating that fact which will speak volumes about your marketing abilities to any new employer.

You can quit at any time but please let us know by email so that we can let someone else at your school carry on.

You are not limited to just your college. You can market anywhere. If you attend some kind of summer travel program or youth program you are welcome to market there as well.

We won't tell you how to market the product. That's up to you. We assume you will probably use one, some, or all of the following (including anything else you can creatively design): Facebook, Twitter, email, sms text blasting, rec center activations, intramural sports activations, etc.

We will supply you with one bottle of Moringa capsules free of charge. Most people feel the effects of Moringa almost right away. So handing out 5-10 capsules as samples is an option for you.

All flyers or coupons that you generate need to be sent to us via email prior to printing so that we can check them for accuracy.

We provide no reimbursement for any promotional costs.

If interested send an email to moringapills at gmail dot com. Provide:

  1. Your Name
  2. Your Campus Address
  3. Your Cell Phone Number
  4. Your Year in School

Include any questions you may have. We will email back your acceptance or rejection. We prefer email communications.

If accepted you will receive by mail your free bottle of Moringa and your identifying code. When people go to buy they will need that code at checkout to receive their $5 discount.

We appreciate your interest in our product and wish you the best with your campaign!
