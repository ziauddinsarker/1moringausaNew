The Moringa Oleifera Tree


Where food sources are scarce, rainfall is minimal and the land is arid and unfriendly, Moringa is a god sent gift of nature. It grows where nothing apparently can thrive, requires no gentle caring hand to rear, and yet has everything to offer. Moringa Oleifera is a plant that has very few competitors. It is a fast growing pleasant looking tree, set apart by its drumstick like pods containing the seeds, turning out leaves during dry season or during times of draught when no plant dares to breathe in the intense heat of the African plains.

Where is Moringa found?

The Moringa Oleifera tree is found in India and also in many parts of Africa.

Moringa Oleifera and Genital Warts

Here is a treatment for Genital Warts Genital warts cannot be cured. The conditions can only be treated to remove the warts. But the virus that causes the warts to appear can never be complexly removed from the body.

Genital warts are small, soft and moist flesh colored growths that appear in the genital area of both the sexes. Genital warts are caused by an infection of the Human Pamplona Virus or the HPV. It is an extremely contagious disease that affects around 5.5 million people in the US alone each year. And when you consider the fact that many people do not even know that they have the disease, the figures can go up even higher. Actually, genital warts is the most wide spread of all the sexually transmitted diseases. These warts often appear in clusters resembling somewhat to a vegetable (cauliflower), and are found on the penis, vagina and anus of the affected person. And sometimes the warts are found even in the thigh area. Genital warts are spread from a person to another through oral, vaginal or anal sex with an infected partner.

Researchers are of the opinion that Moringa Oleifera is able to treat genital warts remarkably well. Genital tract of ovariectomized rats have been treated by an aqueous extract of the Moringa Oleifera roots. This led to biochemical alterations in the genital tract of these ovariectomized rats. This has made researchers believe that the tree can be effective with humans as well.

Health Benefits of Moringa Oleifera

The Moringa Oleifera tree is an excellent source of food rich in protein, Calcium, Magnesium, Potassium, Iron, Vitamin A and Vitamin C. The generous tree also offers its flowers to cure inflammations, pods for joint pains, roots for rheumatism and bark as a digestive. Moreover, the Moringa leaves also have the power to stabilize blood pressure. In short, it is a panacea for ailments and complaints and disorders for the rural population in Africa that is short on funds. Even in death, it sings the song of the land, performing as windbreaks for erosion control.

Why is Moringa Oleifera referred to as the ‘Miracle Tree’?

Obviously because of the several curative benefits of the plant. In fact people in India and Africa have for generations known about the medicinal properties of the tree and have thus used it to cure various ailments. However the knowledge was mainly considered as alternative science and a herbal remedy. Modern medical science has only just begun to accept their long held knowledge. Some are even of the opinion that Moringa Oleifera is probably the most nutritious plant ever discovered.
